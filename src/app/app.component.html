ABOUT US

ABOUT Initiated in 1965, Antaragni has emerged as the biggest college festivals in North India and as one of the most awaited cultural events of the country. With its exciting competitions, splendid performances, and glamorous pro-nites, Antaragni is a spectacle like no other, inviting a crowd of over 1.25 Lakh students from 350 different colleges. Over 55 years of its glorious history, Antaragni has seen many outstanding talents and scintillating performances by the likes of Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy, KSHMR, Adnan Sami, Quintino, Amit Trivedi, Atif Aslam and many more. We have carved a niche for ourselves not only with participants and artists but with the most reputed brands through years of successful and creative collaborations.
